# AI Usage Provision for Engagement Letters

## Sample Language

**Use of Artificial Intelligence Tools.** In performing the Services, the Firm may utilize artificial intelligence tools, including large language models, to assist with legal research, document drafting, contract analysis, and related tasks ("AI Tools"). The Firm's use of AI Tools is subject to the following:

(a) **Attorney Supervision.** All work product generated with the assistance of AI Tools is reviewed, revised, and approved by a licensed attorney before delivery to Client. AI Tools are used to enhance the quality and efficiency of the Firm's services, not to replace attorney judgment.

(b) **Confidentiality.** Client information provided to AI Tools is subject to the same confidentiality obligations set forth in this Agreement. The Firm uses AI Tools that do not retain client data beyond the active session and do not use client inputs for model training purposes.

(c) **Data Handling.** The Firm has reviewed the data handling practices of its AI Tool providers and maintains business data processing agreements where available. The Firm makes reasonable efforts to ensure that client data is protected in accordance with applicable professional responsibility obligations.

(d) **No Additional Fees.** The Firm does not charge separately for the use of AI Tools. Any efficiency gains from AI usage are reflected in the Firm's fee structure and inure to the benefit of Client.

---

*Note: This is sample language for reference. Modify based on jurisdiction, practice area, and client relationship. Have the attorney review and adapt before use.*
